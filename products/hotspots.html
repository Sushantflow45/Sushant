Frequently asked questions

When should I use hotspots instead of product tours?

Use hotspots for self-serve feature discovery without interrupting user workflow. Choose product tours for complex multi-step onboarding sequences.

How quickly can I set up hotspots?

Most teams launch their first hotspot in under 15 minutes using our visual editor. No developer resources required.

Do hotspots affect app performance?

No. Hotspots add less than 50KB and load asynchronously without impacting your application speed.

What results can I expect?

Customers typically see 30% higher click-through rates. Decodo increased trial conversions by 52% using strategic hotspot placement.

Do hotspots work on mobile devices?

Yes, hotspots are touch-friendly and mobile-optimized, unlike hover-dependent tooltips.

Can I customize hotspots to match my brand?

Absolutely. Colors, fonts, positioning, and styling are fully customizable to match your design system.
