Frequently asked questions

How are Monthly Active Users (MAUs) calculated?

We track the number of unique users (that have unique userIDs) who either access any page where Candu is installed or are identified to Candu via data integrations like Segment. Users do not need to view or interact with Candu content to be counted as an MAU. Read how we calculate MAUs.

Why do we price based on MAUs? First, because you have the ability to launch product experiences for all of your users at any time and secondly, our costs scale in accordance with the number of users we need to track and maintain data for.

How does the free trial work?

After an initial kick off call with our team, you will be granted 14 days access to your Candu account, so that you can fully evaluate whether Candu is the best fit for your business.

Our team is here to help with designing & creating Candu experiences and any technical questions that you have too!

How much engineering time is needed to install Candu?

We have tried to make our installation process as low-code as possible by creating a small javascript code snippet that needs to be added to the page(s) you want Candu to be installed.

Typically, this is a light lift task for an engineer (minutes - couple hours max) but more time may be required if you are sending us user data directly via the code snippet or API, rather than via an integration.

How does Candu differ to other digital adoption tools?

Candu stands out from other digital adoption tools by empowering product teams to effortlessly create inline experiences that seamlessly integrate into product pages. These experiences are typically hard-coded, but Candu offers a no-code solution that allows teams to launch and iterate on essential experiences like empty states, getting started pages, and banners with a simple drag, drop, and publish, approach.

Candu also offers a suite of classic in-app messaging options, including overlays like Modals, Pop-Ups, Hotspots and Product Tours. While these options have their merits, Candu's emphasis on inline experiences ensures seamless integration within your product, maintaining a consistent and user-centric UX.

Whether you're a startup looking to enhance user onboarding or an established company seeking to optimize product adoption, Candu scales with your needs, offering flexibility and adaptability for your evolving product strategy.

Does Candu content affect page performance?

Improving page performance is critical to any product, and we measure ourselves by the same standard as internal libraries used by any development team. The Candu SDK is designed to minimize the performance impact of installing it on any page, and we are continually working to increase its performance.
