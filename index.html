Frequently Asked Questions

What is Candu?

Candu is a no-code SaaS platform that lets product and growth teams build, launch, and iterate in-app onboarding, banners, and product tours—without writing code.

Do I need developers to use Candu?

Just once: paste a small React / JavaScript snippet into your app, and every experience can then be created, edited, and shipped from Candu’s drag-and-drop editor.

How much does Candu cost?

Pricing starts with our Starter plan at $199/mo—ideal for early-stage SaaS teams—and scales with usage and advanced features. See details.
